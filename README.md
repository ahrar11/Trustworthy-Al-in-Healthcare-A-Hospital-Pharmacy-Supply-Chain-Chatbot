# Trustworthy-Al-in-Healthcare-A-Hospital-Pharmacy-Supply-Chain-Chatbot
AI chatbot designed to accelerate critical supply-chain workflows in hospital pharmacy operations. The system consolidates fragmented data from ERP systems, warehouse management platforms, FDA Orange Book, and ASHP Guidelines into a single conversational interface. Evaluated on 3 production style modes (Retrieval only, RAG, and LLM-Only)

Led development of a chatbot that unified 6,383 guideline chunks from FDA Orange Book, ASHP guidelines, RxNorm API, and 200k+ inventory lots across 80 locations into a single conversational interface, enabling manual query resolution time from 15 minutes to under 1O seconds
Evaluated 3 production-style modes (retrieval-only, RAG, and LLM-only) over 180 adversarial and ambiguity stress test runs across 30 prompts and multiple temperatures while instrumenting safety guardrails for inventory, drug substitution, and cost queries for frontline clinical users
